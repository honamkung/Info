Ho Namkung

I am a PhD candidate in Biomedical Engineering at Johns Hopkins University School of Medicine. I obtained my Bachelor's degree in Electrical Engineering at POSTECH in 2014.

I am broadly interested in circuit mechanisms of learning and computations relevant for decision making. I have experience with anatomical tracing, optogenetics, in vivo extracellular recording, and data analysis using machine learning.

Research during PhD

During my PhD, I have mainly investigated molecular and neural circuit mechanisms of higher-order brain functions that are frequently impaired across a wide array of psychiatric and neurological disorders.
